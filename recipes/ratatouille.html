Ratatouille

Incredible rustic r atatouille

This recipe will show you how to make incredible ratatouille with healthy ingredients from your garden!

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Eggplant
  • Whole tomatoes
  • Zucchini
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Line a round pan with oil, then spread a layer of tomato sauce inside.
  2. Using a mandolin, slice the eggplant, tomatoes and zuccini.
  3. Repeat these steps until you fill the pan.
  4. Put in oven for 20 mins.
  5. Take out the tray, let cool for 10 mins.
  6. Enjoy!.
